Innovation is transforming ideas into impact. The intersection of innovation and entrepreneurship strategies is the cornerstone for successful local, national, and global partnerships.

The workshop aims to provide an introduction lecture series to innovation methodologies and entrepreneurial knowledge. The workshop program is organized in a lecture series given by Dr. Colleen Stiles, co-Director of Bachelor of Innovation™ Degree, Director of Bachelor of Innovation™ Strategic Alliances, University of Colorado Colorado Springs (UCCS), USA. Many group activities will be organized by participants and for participants to practice the methodologies and to exchange ideas. We will also invite some speakers from industry to share their experiences in the workshop.

This workshop is organized in the framework of collaboration between US and Vietnamese Institutions as a follow-on project of EducationUSA Leadership Institutes program. The workshop is sponsored by EducationUSA, Hanoi University of Science and Technology (HUST), Hanoi University of Science (HUS).

A group of ten students from UCCS Bachelor of Innovation™ will attend and participate in the workshop. Students will provide a unique point of view for participants. We call for the participation of Vietnamese student, junior lecturers and young professionals (especially, those from HUST and HUS) who are interested in teaching and learning innovation, entrepreneurship and in developing the relationship and network with US institutions and individuals. Participants who successfully complete the program will receive a certificate of attendance.
